Cloud Benchmarking

Database systems have gone through lots of transformations over the year. Modern applications are TPS heavy; it is often a challenge for the database to perform better when it scales. Our goal in this project is to analyze the TPC-H queries, execute them on to different cloud database systems, and generate the benchmark.
